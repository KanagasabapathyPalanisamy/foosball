Rule for Game...

Each Team can have minimum of 2 Players and maximum of 3 players Total of 6 member for each game.

Initially we have an available player list according to that Team will be splited Randomly.

You can edit the player list before the game start.

You have to hit the ball to score the goal.

For each Goal the team will rewarded as one point

The team Goals more will be the winner of Each game.
